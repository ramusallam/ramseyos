# Recommendation Letters Playbook

**Workspace:** Sonoma Academy
**Workflow ID:** `sonoma-recommendation-letters`

## Purpose

Draft personalized college recommendation letters that authentically represent each student's strengths, character, and growth as observed through direct classroom experience.

## Inputs

- **Student Name** — Full name of the student.
- **Courses with Student** — Which courses were shared and when.
- **Strengths** — Key academic and personal qualities to emphasize.
- **Anecdotes** (optional) — Specific moments, projects, or interactions that illustrate the student.
- **Target Schools** (optional) — Schools or programs the student is applying to.

## Process

1. Review all provided context about the student.
2. Open with a clear statement of the recommender's relationship to the student.
3. Develop 2-3 body paragraphs that illustrate the student through specific strengths and anecdotes.
4. If target schools are provided, connect the student's qualities to what those programs value.
5. Close with a strong, genuine endorsement.
6. Output a complete letter draft in a professional, warm tone.

## Outputs

- A complete recommendation letter draft in markdown, ready for review and personalization.

## Notes

- Letters must sound authentically like Ramsey, not like generic AI output. Voice and tone matter.
- Anecdotes are the backbone of a strong letter. If none are provided, the draft should flag where specific stories should be inserted.
- Every letter goes through review. The AI draft is a starting point, never a final product.
- Avoid superlatives that aren't backed by specific evidence in the inputs.
